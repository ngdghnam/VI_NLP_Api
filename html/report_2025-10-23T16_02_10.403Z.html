Insights on Virtual Idols

Key Insights

  • BTS has established a systematic digital ecosystem with the official Big Hit Music website serving as the central hub for all official information and global music distribution.
  • The YouTube channel 'BANGTANTV' is a core content platform for driving high fan engagement through diverse content, including solo projects from members like J-Hope and Jin to maintain fan bonds during the group's hiatus.
  • A strategic shift in communication is underway, moving away from the now-inaccessible @BTS_twt Twitter account towards more systematic and managed platforms like Weverse and the official @bts_bighit account.
  • This integrated multi-platform strategy is designed to maintain brand value and effectively manage the global fandom, especially during the group's hiatus.
  • The key platforms in this integrated ecosystem include the Big Hit Music website, YouTube ('BANGTANTV'), Weverse, and Twitter, each playing a strategic role.
