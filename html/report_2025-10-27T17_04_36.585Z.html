Key Insights

  • Sơn Tùng M-TP has successfully transitioned from a singer to a business mogul, establishing M-TP Entertainment to manage his music, branding, and diverse revenue streams.
  • His dominance in the digital space is highlighted by being the first Vietnamese artist to receive a YouTube Diamond Play Button for surpassing 10 million subscribers and cultivating a massive following of over 6.6 million on TikTok.
  • A strategic global approach, including collaborations with international stars like Snoop Dogg and distributing his documentary *Sky Tour: The Movie* on Netflix, has led to unprecedented international chart success on the Billboard Global 200 and Social 50.
  • His diversified business model ensures high revenue through top-tier performance fees, significant YouTube earnings, and major advertising contracts with global brands like Oppo, Shopee, and Gucci.
